About CodeQuiz Pro

CodeQuiz Pro is designed to reinforce your learning through **active recall** and **testing**. We believe the best way to solidify your knowledge is to be challenged on it. Our platform provides a curated set of multiple-choice questions across various programming languages, simulating the types of knowledge checks you'll encounter in interviews or certifications.

**How it Helps:**

  • **Identify Gaps:** Quickly pinpoint areas where your knowledge is weak.
  • **Active Recall:** Forces your brain to retrieve information, making it stick better.
  • **Diverse Topics:** Questions cover fundamental concepts, syntax, and advanced topics.
